The College Experience

I started attending Stevens in the fall of 2017. While there I attended several different clubs and activities. I joined the Stevens Co-op program in order to insure that I would have the necessary job experience to get a job I desired. I was a part of the Stevens Archery club, Gaming club and TCG club. I also attended many campus activities where I gained friends that would last a lifetime.

Japan

One of my best experiences was studying abroad in Tokyo Japan from January 2015 to late April 2015.
